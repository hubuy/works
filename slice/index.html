If we do nothing

  • Problem
  • Insight
  • Solution

On October 2018, the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) released a new report on the impacts of global warming of 1.5 °C above pre-industrial levels.

The report finds that "limiting global warming to 1.5 °C compared with 2 °C would reduce challenging impacts on ecosystems, human health and well-being" and that a 2 °C temperature increase would exacerbate extreme weather, rising sea levels and diminishing Arctic sea ice, coral bleaching, and loss of ecosystems, among other impacts.

To the average person, the report is just another report, full of scientific and political jargon.

While the report is useful to policymakers, the report fails to make it clear to the general public the underlying message -- that we're simply running out of time. 

We created an immersive interactive that shows clearly the chain reaction that results in 1.5 degrees of warming, tangibly showing how it will affect (or is already affecting) the lives of people around the world.

Widening inequality

  • Problem
  • Insight
  • Solution

Inequality is a paradox of our times; in recent decades innovation has exploded, poverty has gone down in every region of the world and emerging markets are booming.

Yet inequalities persist and grow, intersecting and reinforcing each other and perpetuating intergenerational poverty and exclusion.

Inequality is not just about wealth or income. It can also be seen in life expectancy, or how easily a person can get healthcare, quality education or public services. There are inequalities between sexes and social groups. Every facet of inequality greatly hampers our ability to meet the SDGs.

How can we help users understand and measure them all in order to promote sustainable development?

Help users understand the concept by starting the story with urgency, create cliff notes so that facts are easier to digest and easily sharable. Create simple charts that show progress.

A story of survival

  • Problem
  • Insight
  • Solution

One in three women worldwide who have ever been in a relationship will experience violence by their intimate partner at some point in their lives.

 On November 25 - International Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women - their voices must be heard. 

Abuse comes in all forms, from physical to emotional. Even verbally abusive text messages can be considered abuse. Abuse is often left unchecked because of fear, fear of losing her home or her children, fear of retribution, and fear of being labeled a victim. 

We wanted to empower users by showing them how to identify signs of abuse that they or their loved ones are experiencing and offer them a away out.

Multi-Partner Trust Fund Gateway

  • Problem
  • Insight
  • Solution

The Secretary-General has called as part of the UN repositioning to double pooled funding to increase collaboration both within the UN system and outside the UN system working with partners.

The Gateway needs a UX/UI refresh as the user interface is very dated, circa 2003. Gateway was also not device responsive.

Power users appreciate the complex and thorough data available but some of the graphs and charts are just not readable.   

Create an inventory of the components used in the site.
Test if each component is usable or is being used.
Redesign.

Open UNDP

  • Problem
  • Insight
  • Solution

Working in the open: contributing to better development cooperation and results

How can we make comprehensive up-to-date information about more than 5,000 projects available to the public, and contribute to improving the effectiveness and value of development cooperation?

Massive and global data: Open.undp.org is the central point of access to detailed information about UNDP’s more than 5,000 development projects and 8,000 outputs in 170+ countries and territories worldwide.

We used the world map as the basis of information architecture and user interface to show the movement of funds.
